SARA LOU is a Danish Jewelry brand created by Sara Louisa Kramer- With focus on simple, classy designs the collections radiate simplicity, class and raw elegance. All jewelries are made of 925 Sterling Silver and are handcrafted in workshops located in Copenhagen. Each piece is unique - no stones are the same. Therefore each piece is "one of a kind" designed and created specifically by your request. I put everything into my pieces - and i hope you'll love them as much as i do creating them.
